About Us

We at CORONASAHAYATA.in aim to provide the same support for the tender lives to sustain,fight and proactively contain this pandemic . CORONASAHAYATA is not only a project it is a pledge we have taken and we aim to bring together volunteers and leaders from every walk of life and phase to contribute into the greater good of building community spirit to live through these unprecedented times. We found that there was no single portal having all information and people were in panic mode to get necessities, medical help and to know what all have to be done if they are infected.
